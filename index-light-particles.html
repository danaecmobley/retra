About Retra Studio

Retra is a civic-minded design firm that works with organizations to positively impact the fields of urban sustainability, design and technology. We utilize research and design strategy to deliver meaningful outcomes for our clients and their stakeholders. Our goal is to create a positive impact that helps solve some our community’s most significant issues.

We are a certified MBE/WBE in Philadelphia and New Jersey that provides the following services: User Research & Experience Design, Community and Stakeholder Engagement, Visual Design and Branding & Messaging
